---

$$ \huge a,b\ \epsilon\ S\ ;\ a\ *\ b\ \epsilon\ S $$
---
2. Associative Property
$$\huge a\ ,\ b\ \epsilon\ S\ ,\ (\ a\ *\ b\ )\ *\ c\ = a\ *\ (\ b\ *\ c\ ) $$
